1→ Law of complement
2→ Law of union and intersection

1→Law of complement (through vien diagram)

☆(A⋃B)' = A'⋂B'
☆(A⋂B)'=A'⋃B'

2→Law of union and intersection

A⋃(B⋂C) = (A⋃B)⋂(A⋃C)
union is distributive over intersection, vice versa
A⋂(B⋃C)=(A⋂B)⋃(A⋂C)

Addition theorem on Sets(Inclution and exclution principle)

n(A⋃B)=n(A)+n(B)-n(A⋂B)

For three n(A⋃B⋃C)=n(A)+n(B)+n(C)+n(A⋂B⋂C)-n(A⋂B)-n(A⋂C)-n(B⋂C)
